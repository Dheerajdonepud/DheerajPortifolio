About Me

As an enthusiastic Cyber Security practitioner, I bring a robust background in Security Operations Centre (SOC) monitoring, penetration testing, and ethical hacking. My expertise extends to leveraging Python for both automation and security scripting, alongside a strong foundation in full-stack development for building secure applications.

I am well-versed in identifying vulnerabilities, mitigating security risks, and implementing resilient security solutions. My objective is to contribute to a team that values continuous learning and innovation in the cybersecurity landscape.

My practical experience includes assisting in configuring firewall policies and conducting vulnerability assessments at Palo Alto Networks, as well as gaining a solid understanding of the Fortinet Security Fabric and configuring FortiGate firewalls during my Fortinet Network Associate internship.
